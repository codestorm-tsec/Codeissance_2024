Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

+

The hackathon is open for Students from all over India. Students staying far from college can also participate in online mode.

How much will it cost?

+

Registration for Codeissance 2024 is completely free. Accommodation and food charges may apply after teams are shortlisted.

How can I apply?

+

The registration can be done by simply clicking the Devfolio button above and filling up the form, where you will mention you and your team details.

What are the prizes to be won?

+

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

What if I do not know how to code?

+

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level. Beginners are also invited.

Can I attend the hackathon remotely?

+

Yes, you can choose between participating in online or offline mode.
