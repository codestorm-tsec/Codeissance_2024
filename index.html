ABOUT CODEISSANCE 2023
CODEISSANCE 2023 is the annual hackathon organized by TSEC CodeStorm, Thadomal Shahani Engineering College Bandra. It is an offline 24-hr event for innovatively fostering real-world problem-solving skills.

FAQ'S

The hackathon is open for Students from all over India. It is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning and innovating with their ideas.

Registration for TSEC Hacks is completely free, Accomodation and food charges may apply after teams are shortlisted.

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the register button here and filling up the Devfolio form in which you would have to mention you and your team details. Registrations end on 25th September 2023.

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

Team size must be between 2-4 members.
