About Us
TSEC CodeStorm stands out as a unique platform to help develop coding as well as presentation skills, their mantra being “Opportunities don’t happen, you create them”. Their primary goal is to provide a plethora of opportunities for novice coders by conducting various technical events and workshops. The committee succeeds in instigating interest in coding by notifying people with new technologies and their corporate applications by referring to trending technical platforms and frameworks every week. It organizes various workshops and coding competitions to propel the students further into making a glorious career.
OUR PAST EVENTS ------------------
Codeissance 2022 Hackathon
Our prime event was the annual Hackathon which received a great response from the participants as it helped expose them to various technologies while testing their pitching skills. This was a 24- hour ollline hackathon in which students of various colleges participated in domains of AI/ML, Web/App Development and Blockchain.

Technovation 2022
Technovation’22 was the biggest Virtual Project Exhibition of TSEC conducted via the offline mode. It was an all-Mumbai level online project presentation to showcase the students’ innovative and creative projects as well as provide them an opportunity to enhance their presentation skills.

Treasure Hunt 2022
TSEC CodeStorm organized the biggest Treasure Hunt at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College in the offline mode after a period of two years on 4th March 2022. This event was exclusively for the First-Year students of TSEC. The event was a success with the participation of 43 teams competing against each other to showcase their talent.
